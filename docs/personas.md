# User Personas

## Persona 1: HR Administrator (Amanda)

### Demographics
- **Name:** Amanda Rodriguez
- **Age:** 35
- **Role:** HR_ADMIN
- **Experience:** 10 years in HR management
- **Education:** MBA in Human Resources
- **Location:** Headquarters office

### Professional Background
- Senior HR Manager at a tech company
- Manages team of 5 recruiters
- Responsible for hiring 50+ positions per year
- Oversees entire recruitment process
- Reports to Chief People Officer

### Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** Optimize recruitment process efficiency
- **Secondary Goals:**
  - Ensure compliance with hiring regulations
  - Maintain data quality and security
  - Generate executive reports on hiring metrics
  - Reduce time-to-hire from 45 to 30 days

### Pain Points
- Overwhelmed by volume of CVs (200+ per position)
- Difficulty tracking recruiter performance
- Manual CV review is time-consuming
- Inconsistent candidate evaluations across team
- Limited visibility into hiring pipeline

### Technical Proficiency
- **Level:** Intermediate
- **Systems Used:** ATS, HRIS, Excel, Email
- **Comfort with new tech:** Moderate (needs training)

### Behavioral Traits
- Detail-oriented and organized
- Data-driven decision maker
- Values security and compliance
- Appreciates automation
- Prefers dashboards and visual reports

### Use Case Scenarios
1. **Weekly Pipeline Review:** Checks dashboard every Monday morning to review all active job positions, candidate counts, and average scores
2. **Team Management:** Creates new recruiter accounts, assigns permissions, monitors their activities through audit logs
3. **Executive Reporting:** Generates monthly reports on hiring metrics to present to leadership team
4. **Quality Assurance:** Reviews top-ranked candidates to ensure algorithm accuracy

### User Stories
- As Amanda, I want to view all system users so that I can manage team access
- As Amanda, I want to see audit logs so that I can track all system activities
- As Amanda, I want comprehensive reports so that I can present metrics to executives
- As Amanda, I want to manage job positions so that I can oversee all recruitment activities

### Quote
> "I need a system that saves my team time while maintaining the quality of our candidate selections. Data and accountability are crucial."

## Persona 2: HR Recruiter (James)

### Demographics
- **Name:** James Chen
- **Age:** 28
- **Role:** HR_RECRUITER
- **Experience:** 4 years in recruitment
- **Education:** Bachelor's in Business Administration
- **Location:** Remote worker

### Professional Background
- Technical recruiter specializing in software roles
- Handles 8-10 active job openings simultaneously
- Conducts initial candidate screenings
- Coordinates with hiring managers
- Focuses on developer and engineer roles

### Goals & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** Find best candidates quickly
- **Secondary Goals:**
  - Build strong candidate pipeline
  - Improve candidate experience
  - Meet hiring quotas (5 hires per quarter)
  - Reduce manual screening time

### Pain Points
- Spends 60% of time reading CVs
- Hard to compare candidates objectively
- Misses qualified candidates in large applicant pools
- Difficult to justify ranking decisions to hiring managers
- Repetitive manual data entry

### Technical Proficiency
- **Level:** High
- **Systems Used:** Multiple ATS systems, LinkedIn, Google Suite
- **Comfort with new tech:** Very comfortable

### Behavioral Traits
- Fast-paced worker
- Appreciates automation and shortcuts
- Results-oriented
- Good communicator
- Likes clear, actionable information

### Use Case Scenarios
1. **Monday Morning Bulk Upload:** Receives 50 CVs from job board, uploads them all at once to system, reviews parsing results
2. **Candidate Ranking:** Opens new software engineer position, clicks "Rank Candidates" button, reviews top 10 matches with explanations
3. **Detailed Review:** Clicks on top-ranked candidate to see full CV details, parsed skills, and matching explanation
4. **Position Creation:** Creates new job posting with required skills, minimum experience, and keywords based on hiring manager input

### User Stories
- As James, I want to upload multiple CVs at once so that I can process large applicant pools efficiently
- As James, I want to see ranked candidate lists so that I can quickly identify best matches
- As James, I want explanations for rankings so that I can justify recommendations to hiring managers
- As James, I want to create job positions so that I can start matching candidates immediately
- As James, I want to view candidate details so that I can make informed decisions
- As James, I want to see skills frequency reports so that I can understand market trends

### Quote
> "I just want to spend less time reading CVs and more time talking to great candidates. Show me who's qualified and why."
