## Persona 1: HR_ADMIN (Administrator)

### Personal Profile
**Name:** Rachel Cohen  
**Age:** 38  
**Job Title:** HR Director  
**Experience:** 12 years in HR management  
**Education:** MBA in Human Resource Management  
**Location:** Tel Aviv, Israel  
**Tech Savviness:** High (4/5)

### Photo Description
Professional woman in business attire, confident demeanor, working at a modern office desk with multiple monitors.

---

### Role Overview
Rachel is an HR Director responsible for overseeing the entire recruitment process, managing the HR team, ensuring compliance, and making strategic hiring decisions based on data analytics.

### Goals & Objectives
1. **Strategic Planning:**
   - Analyze hiring trends to make data-driven decisions
   - Identify skills gaps in the organization
   - Optimize recruitment budget and resources

2. **Team Management:**
   - Manage and support HR recruiters
   - Ensure consistent evaluation processes
   - Monitor team performance and productivity

3. **Compliance & Security:**
   - Maintain audit trails for legal compliance
   - Ensure data privacy and security
   - Monitor system usage and prevent misuse

4. **Reporting:**
   - Generate executive reports for leadership
   - Track key recruitment metrics (time-to-hire, cost-per-hire)
   - Benchmark against industry standards

---

### Responsibilities in System
✅ **Full System Access** - Access to all features and data  
✅ **User Management** - Create, edit, and deactivate user accounts  
✅ **View All Reports** - Access comprehensive analytics and dashboards  
✅ **Manage Job Positions** - Oversight of all active and closed positions  
✅ **View Audit Logs** - Monitor all system activities  
✅ **System Configuration** - Adjust settings and permissions  
✅ **Export Data** - Generate reports and export to CSV/PDF  

---

### Capabilities & Permissions

| Feature | Access Level | Actions |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Authentication** | Full | Login, logout, password reset |
| **User Management** | Admin | Create, read, update, delete users |
| **CV Management** | View All | Access all uploaded CVs |
| **Job Positions** | Full Control | Create, edit, close, delete jobs |
| **Matching/Ranking** | View Results | See rankings, approve recommendations |
| **Reports** | Full Access | All reports, custom date ranges, export |
| **Audit Logs** | Read Only | View, search, filter, export logs |
| **Dashboard** | Full | All widgets, custom configuration |

---

### Pain Points
1. **Data Overload:** Too much data without actionable insights
2. **Compliance Risk:** Difficulty tracking who accessed what data
3. **Inconsistent Evaluations:** Different recruiters use different criteria
4. **Slow Reporting:** Manual report generation takes hours
5. **Limited Visibility:** Can't see real-time recruitment pipeline status
6. **Security Concerns:** Worried about data breaches and unauthorized access

---

### Needs from System
1. **Comprehensive Dashboards** - At-a-glance view of recruitment status
2. **Advanced Analytics** - Trend analysis, predictive insights
3. **Audit Trail** - Complete logging of all user actions
4. **Role Management** - Granular permission control
5. **Automated Reports** - Scheduled weekly/monthly reports
6. **Data Security** - Encryption, access controls, compliance features
7. **Export Capabilities** - Download data for presentations

---

3. Review pipeline metrics (candidates in process, time-to-hire)
4. Check audit logs for any unusual activity

**Weekly Activities:**
1. Generate executive report for leadership meeting

**Monthly Tasks:**
1. Comprehensive recruitment performance review
2. Budget analysis and forecasting
3. Compliance audit preparation

5. System configuration updates

### Success Metrics

## Persona 2: HR_RECRUITER (Recruiter)

### Personal Profile
**Name:** David Levi  
**Age:** 29  
**Job Title:** Senior HR Recruiter  
**Experience:** 5 years in technical recruitment  
**Education:** B.A. in Business Administration  
**Location:** Haifa, Israel  
**Tech Savviness:** Medium (3/5)

### Photo Description
Young professional in smart-casual attire, friendly and approachable, reviewing documents at a desk with a laptop and coffee.

---

### Role Overview
David is a hands-on recruiter responsible for day-to-day talent acquisition activities. He works directly with hiring managers, screens candidates, manages the interview process, and makes hiring recommendations.

---

### Goals & Objectives
1. **Efficient Screening:**
   - Quickly identify top candidates from large applicant pools
   - Reduce time spent on manual CV review
   - Focus on high-value candidates

2. **Quality Hiring:**
   - Match candidates accurately to job requirements
   - Minimize false positives (wrong candidates advancing)
   - Improve offer acceptance rates

3. **Process Improvement:**
   - Streamline repetitive tasks
   - Maintain organized candidate records
   - Track candidate communication and status

4. **Stakeholder Satisfaction:**
   - Meet hiring manager expectations
   - Fill positions quickly without compromising quality
   - Provide data-backed recommendations

---

### Responsibilities in System
✅ **Upload CVs** - Batch upload multiple candidate files  
✅ **Create Job Positions** - Define requirements and skills  
✅ **Run Candidate Ranking** - Execute matching algorithm  
✅ **View Reports** - Access recruitment-specific analytics  
✅ **Manage Candidates** - Update statuses, add notes  
✅ **Export Rankings** - Share results with hiring managers  

---

### Capabilities & Permissions

| Feature | Access Level | Actions |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Authentication** | User | Login, logout, password reset |
| **User Management** | None | View own profile only |
| **CV Management** | Full | Upload, view, parse, organize CVs |
| **Job Positions** | Create/Edit Own | Create jobs, edit own positions |
| **Matching/Ranking** | Full | Run ranking, view scores, explanations |
| **Reports** | Limited | View own recruitment metrics |
| **Audit Logs** | None | No access |
| **Dashboard** | Standard | Own activities and assignments |

---

### Pain Points
1. **Volume Overload:** Receives 100+ CVs per position
2. **Manual Screening:** Spends 60% of time on initial CV review
3. **Inconsistent Format:** CVs come in different formats and structures
4. **Subjectivity:** Hard to objectively compare candidates
5. **Missing Information:** CVs often lack key details
6. **Time Pressure:** Multiple open positions with tight deadlines
7. **Communication Gap:** Difficulty explaining rankings to hiring managers

---

### Needs from System
1. **Fast CV Upload** - Drag-and-drop, batch processing
2. **Automatic Parsing** - Extract skills, experience, education
3. **Smart Ranking** - Objective, explainable candidate scores
4. **Skills Matching** - Clear visualization of skill gaps
5. **Quick Search** - Find candidates by specific criteria
6. **Export to Hiring Managers** - Shareable shortlists
7. **Mobile-Friendly** - Review candidates on the go

---

### Typical User Journey

**When New Position Opens:**
1. Log in to system
2. Create new job position with required skills
3. Upload CVs from various sources (email, LinkedIn, job boards)
4. Review auto-parsed candidate profiles
5. Run ranking algorithm for the position
6. Review top 10 candidates
7. Export shortlist and share with hiring manager

**Daily Workflow:**
1. Check dashboard for new CVs uploaded
2. Review candidates flagged by system
3. Update candidate statuses (interviewed, rejected, offer)
4. Run quick reports on pipeline health
5. Upload additional CVs as they arrive

**End of Week:**
1. Generate weekly pipeline report
2. Review candidates moving to next stages
3. Identify bottlenecks in process
4. Plan interviews for following week

---

### Success Metrics
- **Time Savings:** 50% reduction in screening time
- **Quality of Hire:** 85%+ candidate-job match accuracy
- **Speed:** Shortlist delivered within 24 hours of job posting
- **Hiring Manager Satisfaction:** 4.5/5 rating
- **Offer Acceptance:** 70%+ acceptance rate

---

### Quotes
> "I need the system to do the heavy lifting on initial screening so I can focus on building relationships with top candidates."

> "Give me confidence that the top-ranked candidate is actually the best fit, not just the one with the most keywords."

> "I'm juggling 8 open positions right now. I need speed without sacrificing quality."

## Design Implications

### For HR_ADMIN:
- **Dashboard:** Focus on high-level KPIs, trends, alerts
- **Navigation:** Quick access to reports and user management
- **Visualizations:** Charts, graphs, heat maps
- **Exports:** PDF, CSV, Excel formats
- **Security:** Two-factor authentication, session timeout

### For HR_RECRUITER:
- **Dashboard:** Focus on active jobs, recent uploads, pending tasks
- **Navigation:** Streamlined for repetitive tasks
- **Visualizations:** Candidate cards, match scores, skill bars
- **Bulk Actions:** Upload multiple CVs, bulk status updates
- **Notifications:** Alerts for new candidates, matches
